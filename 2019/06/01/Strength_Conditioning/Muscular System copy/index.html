Golgi Tendon Organs (GTO)

  • 􏰀 located in tendons near the myotendinous junction.
  • 􏰀 They occur in series (i.e., attached end to end) with extrafusal muscle fibers.
  • 􏰀 When an extremely heavy load is placed on the muscle, discharge of the GTO occurs.
  • 􏰀 sensory neuron of the GTO activates an inhibitory interneuron in the spinal cord, which in turn synapses with and inhibits a motor neuron serving the same muscle.
